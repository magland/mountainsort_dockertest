View the results from the paper:

First download the appropriate files by doing:
prv-download results/ms2mn_probe.mv2 --server=http://datalaboratory.org:8005

Then open the viewer
mountainview results/ms2mn_probe.mv2

Or directly
mountainview --samplerate=30000 --raw=probe/raw.mda.prv --firings=results/firings_ms2mn_probe.mda.prv --geom=probe/geom.csv
